Security and Compliance

Last updated: 2026-03-17

Account protection

  • Use strong Telegram account security and 2FA.
  • Verify links before opening trading pages.
  • Never share private keys, seed phrases, or OTP codes.

Operational controls

  • Transaction monitoring and anti-abuse controls.
  • Rate limits and behavior anomaly detection.
  • Incident response and channel verification notices.

Compliance principles

Crypto Bot users must follow local regulations, sanctions rules, and tax obligations in their jurisdictions.
