How P2P trading works

Telegram P2P allows users to trade directly with each other without relying on a centralized exchange interface. You can compare offers, choose payment methods, and complete transactions inside Telegram.

  2. Choose the best offer based on price and limits
  3. Complete payment and confirm transaction

Why use P2P in Telegram

  • Flexible payment methods
  • Access to local market pricing
  • Direct user-to-user interaction
  • Integrated workflow inside Telegram

When P2P is useful

P2P trading is useful when you want to buy crypto using local payment methods or sell assets directly to other users without going through traditional exchange orders.
