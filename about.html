About Crypto Bot

Understanding how crypto works inside Telegram.

What is Crypto Bot

Crypto Bot is a Telegram-based platform that allows users to manage cryptocurrency directly inside messaging workflows. It combines wallet functionality, trading tools, P2P exchange, and payment features into a single interface.

Mission

The goal is to simplify how people interact with crypto by reducing friction and removing the need for multiple apps. Users can store, trade, and transfer assets without leaving Telegram.

Core products

  • Wallet — store and manage crypto balances
  • P2P marketplace — buy and sell with real offers
  • Exchange — market and limit trading
  • Crypto Pay — invoices and payment flows

Who it is for

  • Beginners who want a simple entry point into crypto
  • Users who prefer Telegram-native workflows
  • Traders combining wallet, exchange, and P2P flows
  • Businesses accepting crypto payments

How everything connects

Crypto Bot is not a single feature but a connected ecosystem. Users can move funds between wallet, trading tools, and payment flows depending on their needs.
