Crypto Pay and Invoices

Accept crypto payments in Telegram and create invoice flows for creators, merchants, and online businesses.

What Crypto Pay helps you do

Crypto Pay is designed for businesses and individuals who want to receive payments through a Telegram-native crypto workflow. Instead of relying only on traditional payment tools, you can generate invoices, share payment links, and simplify crypto-based payment collection.

  • Create single-payment invoices for services or products
  • Use payment links for digital offers and online sales
  • Support subscription or recurring payment workflows
  • Keep payment activity close to your Telegram operations

Who Crypto Pay is for

Crypto Pay is useful for creators, freelancers, merchants, agencies, community owners, and online businesses that want to accept cryptocurrency in a more direct way.

  • Creators selling content or digital access
  • Freelancers invoicing for services
  • Merchants accepting crypto payments online
  • Businesses automating payment flows inside Telegram

Why invoices and payment links matter

Invoices and payment links make it easier to standardize how customers pay. Instead of manually sharing wallet details every time, you can guide users through a cleaner payment flow and reduce friction.

How Crypto Pay fits with wallet and exchange

Crypto Pay works best when connected to a broader crypto workflow. You can receive funds, hold them in a wallet, and move into exchange or P2P flows depending on how you want to manage liquidity and operations.
